A Diagnostic Evaluation of Small Language Models for Offline Writing-Feedback Tutoring in Brazilian Portuguese

Structural conformance and a repertoire of mediation functions, under public-school infrastructure constraints.

Status. This manuscript is currently under revision for submission to the Journal of the Brazilian Computer Society (JBCS / SBC). The previous interactive report has been retired because it no longer reflects the current version of the study. A complete report will be republished here once the article is accepted.

The study evaluates eight open Small Language Models, run locally and offline, on their ability to give cohesion-and-coherence writing feedback to lower-secondary students, and complements the technical benchmark with a specialist human reference coded into eight mediation functions that ground its pedagogical dimension.
